Our Story

At Bread & Butter, baking is more than a craft — it’s a tradition filled with passion and warmth.

Every loaf, pastry, and dessert is carefully prepared using fresh ingredients and time-honored techniques.

Our bakery was created to bring people together, to share moments over freshly baked bread, sweet pastries, and comforting aromas.

From the first knead of dough to the final golden crust, we bake every product with love and dedication.
